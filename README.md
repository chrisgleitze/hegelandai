# Hegel & AI

A small Next.js/Tailwind one-pager mirroring the structure of `projects/kantandai`, adapted for **Hegel and AI**.

## Contents

- Hero page with Hegel portrait
- Literature overview parsed from `HEGEL_AI_LITERATURE_2026_claude.md`
- 5 book/core-text suggestions and 5 article/paper suggestions
- German legal pages (`/impressum`, `/datenschutz`) kept from the reference project

## Literature focus

The shortlist emphasizes dialectic, recognition, objective spirit, institutions, responsibility, and the question whether AI systems can be understood as mind-like, subject-like, or merely as mediated tools inside human practices.
